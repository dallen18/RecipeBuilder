Basic Biscuits

Nov 20, 2023

Prep: 15 mins

Cook: 10 mins

Yields: 10 Biscuits

Ingredients

  • 01.

    Gather all ingredients and preheat oven to 450 degrees F.

  • 02.

    In a large mixing bowl sift together flour, baking powder and salt. Cut in shortening with fork or pastry blender until mixture resembles coarse crumbs.

  • 03.

    Pour milk into flour mixture while stirring with a fork. Mix in milk until dough is soft, moist and pulls away from the side of the bowl.

  • 04.

    Turn dough out onto a lightly floured surface and knead dough briefly, 5 to 7 times.

  • 05.

    Roll dough out into a 1/2 inch thick sheet and cut out biscuits with a floured cookie cutter. Press together unused dough and repeat rolling and cutting procedure.

  • 06.

    Place biscuits on ungreased baking sheets and bake in preheated oven until golden brown, about 10 minutes.Enjoy!
