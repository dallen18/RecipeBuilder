Buttermilk Pumpkin Pancakes

Nov 17, 2023

Prep: 10 mins

Cook: 5 mins

Yields: 16 Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 01.

    Whisk flour, sugar, pumpkin pie spice, baking powder, baking soda, and salt together in a large bowl. Whisk in buttermilk, pumpkin, oil, eggs, and vanilla extract until well blended.

  • 02.

    Spray a large nonstick griddle or skillet with cooking spray. Heat over medium heat.

  • 03.

    Pour batter by scant 1/3 cupfuls into the hot skillet, working in batches. Cook until bubbles form on the surface of pancakes and bottoms are lightly browned, about 1 1/2 minutes per side.

  • 04.

    Repeat with remaining batter, spraying the skillet with cooking spray between batches as needed. Serve warm.
